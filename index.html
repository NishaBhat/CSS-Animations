Hover over me : Skew

  • Skew(x-axis,y-axis) - For skewing along X & Y axis
  • SkewX(x-axis) - This can take both positive and negative values to skew in the positive or negative X direction

Hover over me : Scale

  • Scale(width,height) - Increases or decreases the size of an element
  • scaleX and scaleY can be used to scale only along X or Y axis
  • Eg: Scale(1,1) - Will maintain the original height/width
  • Eg: Scale(2,2) - Doubles the height/width
  • Eg: Scale(0.5,0.5) - Will decrease the height/width by 50%

Hover over me : Rotate

  • Rotate over an element clockwise or counter-clockwise
  • Rotate(degrees) - positive value rotates clockwise
  • Rotate(degrees) - negative value rotates counter-clockwise

Hover over me : Translate

  • Moves an element from its current position
  • Translate(x-axis,y-axis)
  • TranslateX(x-axis) - To move only along x-axis. Eg: TranslateX(50px)
  • TranslateY(y-axis) - To move only along y-axis. With negative values you can move along the negative y direction
